ï»¿ HB4364 S MIL AMT #1 3-1Wolfe 7816 The Committee on Military moved to amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting therefor a new title, to read as follows:Eng. Com. Sub. for House Bill 4364—A Bill to amend and reenact §1-1-4 and §49-4-701 of the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended; and to amend the code by adding a new section, designated §1-1-6, relating to concurrent jurisdiction over land acquired by the United States for military purposes; clarifying execution of process on land acquired by the United States; providing for process for establishment of concurrent jurisdiction over land acquired by the United States for military purposes; authorizing cooperation agreements between state, local, and federal agencies; and providing for concurrent jurisdiction of circuit courts with U.S. District Courts in the state over certain juvenile delinquency and status offenses.  AdoptedRejected
