Maynard[Passed March 14, 2026; in effect 90 days from passage (June 12, 2026)] AN ACT to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, by adding a new section, designated §24A-5-2c, relating to agreements between solid waste motor carriers and commercial customers.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 5. POWERS AND DUTIES OF COMMISSION. §24A-5-2c. Agreements between solid waste motor carriers and commercial customers. All agreements between a solid waste motor carrier and a commercial customer shall be in writing and shall be drafted in accordance with the commission’s rules and general orders governing such agreements: Provided, That such agreements may have a term of more than one year. The Clerk of the Senate and the Clerk of the House of Delegates hereby certify that the foregoing bill is correctly enrolled.  ...............................................................
