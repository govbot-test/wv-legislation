Office of Miners’ Health, Safety, and Training; Administration; Enforcement. §22A-1-10a. Salary increase for state mine inspectors and other inspectors. State mine inspectors, including electrical inspectors, underground mine inspectors, surface mine inspectors, and any other class of inspector described in this chapter, shall receive a $5,000 salary increase on the effective date of this section. The Clerk of the Senate and the Clerk of the House of Delegates hereby certify that the foregoing bill is correctly enrolled.  ...............................................................
