Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE Concurrent RESOLUTION 23(By Delegates Statler, Hillenbrand)[Originating in the Committee on Education; Reported on February 25, 2026] Requesting the Joint Committee on Government and Finance to conduct a study investigating the most appropriate method of providing more Firefighter I and II instructional programs across the state.Whereas, there has been testimony before the Education Committee that some counties lack either instructors or the money to pay for the instruction;Whereas, in many counties, firefighters are unpaid volunteers;Whereas, there has been testimony before the Education Committee that the lack of first responders is resulting in a delayed or no response to 911 calls;Whereas, there is a need to increase our state’s firefighter workforceResolved by the House of Delegates:That the Joint Committee on Government and Finance is hereby requested to conduct a study investigating the areas in the state that are struggling to recruit, retain, and instruct firefighters and the appropriate method of providing more instructional opportunities of Fire Fighter I and II to individuals across the state; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the study investigates the areas in the state that have issues with recruitment, retainment, and training of firefighters; and, be itFurther Resolved,That the study includes the appropriate amount and location of Fire Fighter Education Programs; and, be itFurther Resolved,That the study determines the appropriate number of instructors to be employed across the state; and, be itFurther Resolved,That the study includes the estimated costs of establishing and sustaining the programs; and, be it Further Resolved,That the expenses necessary to conduct this study, prepare a report, and to draft necessary legislation be paid from legislative appropriations to the Joint Committee on Government and Finance.
