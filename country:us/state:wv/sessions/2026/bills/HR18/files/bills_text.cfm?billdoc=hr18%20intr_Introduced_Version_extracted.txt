Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE RESOLUTION 18(By Delegates Eldridge, Hillenbrand, Sheedy, D. Smith, Hite, Anderson, Street, Ridenour, Clay, Pritt, and Watt)[Introduced February 19, 2026; referred to the Committee on Rules] Congratulating the West Virginia All Airborne Chapter of the 82nd Airborne Association for being named the Chapter of the Year for 2025.Whereas, The West Virginia All Airborne Chapter of the 82nd Airborne Association was named the Chapter of the Year for 2025; and Whereas, Significant achievements of the Chapter that led to the Chapter of the Year for 2025, as described by the Paraglide newsletter/ magazine, included:(1) Its "82nd Airborne Mustang" was determined a mobile symbol of the association's Airborne spirit;(2) The Chapter’s disaster relief support effort in California and Tennessee and the Veterans Home in Barboursville;(3) Fund raising activities for the annual Bridge Day celebration in Fayetteville and Christmas dinner at Glade Springs Resort; and (4) The recognition for resilience and outreach and "All the Way" spirit; therefore, be it.Resolved by the House of Delegates:That the House of Delegates congratulates the West Virginia All Airborne Chapter of the 82nd Airborne Association for being named the Chapter of the Year for 2025; and, be it Further Resolved, That the Clerk forward a copy of this resolution to the 82d Airborne Division Association Inc., West Virgina Chapter in Clarksburg, West Virginia.
