HB4012 HFA Hansen 2-5 revised

Altizer – 3259

 

Delegate Hansen moves to amend Com Sub for HB4012 on page 9, Section 11a, line 62, following the period by inserting a new subsection, to read as follows:

"(j) As part of the annual management summary report required under §24-1-1(d) of this code, the commission shall include a section evaluating the implementation and effectiveness of advanced transmission technologies in improving electric transmission efficiency, including an analysis of the costs incurred by, or savings realized by, ratepayers resulting from the use of such technologies."
