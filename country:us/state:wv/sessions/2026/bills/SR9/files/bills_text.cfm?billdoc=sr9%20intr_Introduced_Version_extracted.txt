Red = new code to be enacted SENATE RESOLUTION 9(By Senators Smith (Mr. President) and Hamilton)[Introduced January 21, 2026] Designating January 22, 2026, as Tucker County Day at the Legislature.Whereas, Tucker County, established in 1856, is a historic and vibrant county and is home to five incorporated communities: Parsons, Davis, Thomas, Hendricks, and Hambleton; andWhereas, 2026 marks the 170th anniversary of Tucker County and the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United States, celebrating the county’s enduring contributions within the nation’s history; andWhereas, The City of Parsons, the county seat of Tucker County, is located at the confluence of rivers and offers family-friendly festivals, impressive public parks, and a small-town atmosphere; andWhereas, Continued progress on the construction of Corridor H (Route 48) through Tucker County enhances regional connectivity, improves transportation safety, and provides benefits to residents and travelers; andWhereas, Tucker County is an important contributor to the state in the industries of tourism and outdoor recreation, as well as lumber and wood products; andWhereas, Tucker County is home to two state parks, Blackwater Falls and Canaan Valley; two designated wilderness areas, Dolly Sods and Otter Creek; and a portion of the Monongahela National Forest; andWhereas, Blackwater Canyon, one of the most iconic and scenic landscapes in West Virginia, was formerly privately owned and now has been made part of the Monongahela National Forest; andWhereas, Tucker County has a rich history, a unique culture, and incredible natural beauty that attracts visitors who seek authentic experiences; andWhereas, The citizens of Tucker County travel to the state capitol on an annual basis during the legislative session to participate in the democratic process, in which they share their accomplishments, aspirations, and concerns with lawmakers; andWhereas, It is fitting to recognize Tucker County for its history, culture, outdoor recreation, and inspiring future in the State of West Virginia; therefore, be itResolved by the Senate:That the Senate hereby designates January 22, 2026, as Tucker County Day at the Legislature; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Senate acknowledges the many important contributions the citizens of Tucker County make in the State of West Virginia and encourages those citizens to continue to participate in the democratic process; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk is hereby directed to forward a copy of this resolution to the representatives from Tucker County.
