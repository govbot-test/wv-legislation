Red = new code to be enacted HOUSE RESOLUTION 23(By Speaker Roger Hanshaw)[Introduced March 14, 2026] Calling for Interim Committee Meetings of the House of Delegates during calendar year 2026.Whereas, the House of Delegates desires to hold interim meetings in addition to those already established by the Joint Committee on Government and Finance and published on the website of the West Virginia Legislature; andWhereas, interim meetings are as integral part of the legislative process affording House members opportunities to frame issues, investigate matters, establish pertinent facts, and develop policy thereby shaping legislative intent and direction for the upcoming legislative session; and be itResolved by the West Virginia House of Delegates That the dates, times and locations of said House interim meetings shall be at the call of the Speaker and public notice will be given thereof; and be itFurther Resolved, that those delegates in attendance as said House Interim Meetings shall be paid for their legislative services, together with expenses, in accordance with West Virginia Code §4-2A-1, et. seq.
