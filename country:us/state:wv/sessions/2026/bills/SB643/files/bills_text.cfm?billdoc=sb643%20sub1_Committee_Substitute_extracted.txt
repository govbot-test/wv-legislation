Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 12. WEST VIRGINIA SUPREME COURT OF APPEALS PUBLIC CAMPAIGN FINANCING PILOT PROGRAM. §3-12-1. Short title. Termination of the West Virginia Supreme Court of Appeals Public Campaign Financing Program. This article is known as the "West Virginia Supreme Court of Appeals Public Campaign Financing Program." The West Virginia Supreme Court of Appeals Campaign Financing Pilot Program established pursuant to this article will terminate on June 30, 2026: Provided, That on or after July 1, 2026, any unexpended balance remaining in the Supreme Court of Appeals Public Campaign Financing Fund shall be transferred to the General Revenue Fund. §3-12-2.
