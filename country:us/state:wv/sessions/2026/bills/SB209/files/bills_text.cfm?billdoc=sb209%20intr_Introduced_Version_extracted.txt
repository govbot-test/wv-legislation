Red = new code to be enacted WEST VIRGINIA LEGISLATURE2026 REGULAR SESSION FISCAL NOTE IntroducedSenate Bill 209By Senator Helton[Introduced January 14, 2026; referredto the Committee on Government Organization; and then to the Committee on Finance]A BILL to amend the Code of West Virginia, 1931, as amended, by adding a new article, designated §16-9H-1, relating to the creation of the Vape Regulation and Enforcement Act; providing for definitions; and empowering the Alcohol Beverage Control Administration to regulate the sale of vapes and vape cartridges.Be it enacted by the Legislature of West Virginia: ARTICLE 9H. vape regulation and enforcement act. §16-9H-1. Relating to the regulation and enforcement of vape products. (a) This article is designed to regulate any business in this state that sells "vapes" or "vape cartridges," defined as any device that is used to inhale vapor containing nicotine and flavoring.(b) Any business in this state which intends to sell vapes or vape cartridges shall obtain a license to sell such products. (1) The license shall contain identifying information on the owner and responsible person that shall be present when an inspection takes place on a business selling vapes or vape cartridges.(2) The license shall contain all demographic information on both the owner and operator of a business selling vapes or vape cartridges.(3) A background check shall be conducted on any owner or operator of a business that sells vapes or vape cartridges.(c) All other requirements under this article shall otherwise follow the requirements to sell alcohol in this state.(d) The Alcohol Beverage Control Administration is hereby established as the regulating entity for any business selling vapes or vape cartridges in this state.(1) The Alcohol Beverage Control Administration shall maintain the authority to establish a state registry of vapes, vape cartridges, and vape products subject to criteria otherwise set forth in this code, including but not limited to packaging restrictions, product design controls, and marketing and packaging restrictions.(2) The Alcohol Beverage Control Administration shall have rulemaking authority to regulate the sale of vapes and vape cartridges in this state.(e) Advertising for the sale of vapes and vape cartridges shall follow the same regulations as otherwise set forth for Limited Video Lottery (LVL) terminals. (f) The Alcohol Beverage Control Administration is empowered to create penalties for violations by an owner, operator, or employee who sells vapes and vape cartridges. Such penalties may apply to the owner and operator based upon an employees’ actions.  The commissioner of the Alcohol Beverage Control Administration shall have the discretion to suspend or revoke the license of a business who sells vapes or vape cartridges.NOTE: The purpose of this bill is to regulate the sale of vape products.
