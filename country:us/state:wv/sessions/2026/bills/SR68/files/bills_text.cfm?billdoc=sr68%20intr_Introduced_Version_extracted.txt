Maynard, Z. Maynard, Morris, Oliverio, Phillips, Queen, Roberts, Rose, Rucker, Takubo, Tarr, Taylor, Thorne, Weld, Willis, Woelfel and Woodrum)	[Introduced March 14, 2026]	 Recognizing the dedicated public service of the Honorable Charles Houston Clements.Whereas, The Honorable Charles Houston Clements was born August 21, 1943, son of the late Charles and Margaret Clements; andWhereas, The Honorable Charles Houston Clements earned his A.B. in Mathematics from West Virginia University; andWhereas, The Honorable Charles Houston Clements was elected to the West Virginia House of Delegates in 1994, and served on term; andWhereas, The Honorable Charles Houston Clements was appointed to the West Virginia Senate on January 28, 2017, and was elected to that seat in 2019, and reelected in 2023; andWhereas, During his tenure in the Senate, the Honorable Charles Houston Clements served as Chair of the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure, 84th, 85th, and 86th Legislatures; andWhereas, The Honorable Charles Houston Clements has decided not to seek reelection to the Senate in 2026; therefore, be itResolved by the Senate:That the Senate hereby recognizes the dedicated public service of the Honorable Charles Houston Clements; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Senate extends its sincere appreciation and gratitude to the Honorable Charles Houston Clements for his dedication and commitment to serving the citizens of West Virginia; and, be itFurther Resolved, That the Clerk is hereby directed to forward a copy of this resolution to the Honorable Charles Houston Clements.
